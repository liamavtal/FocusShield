Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 31, 2025

Summary: FocusGuard stores your blocked sites list and settings locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data.

Information We Store

  • Blocked Websites: The list of websites you choose to block
  • Settings: Your preferences (focus mode state, dark mode, etc.)
  • Statistics: Count of blocks performed (stored locally)
  • License Key (Pro): Your license key for Pro features

Data Storage

All data is stored locally using Chrome's storage API:

  • Free users: Data stored in local browser storage only
  • Pro users: Settings can sync across devices via Chrome Sync

What We Don't Collect

  • Browsing history
  • Personal information
  • Analytics or tracking data
  • Cookies from third parties

Third-Party Services

Pro license validation uses LemonSqueezy's API. This only transmits your license key for validation - no personal data is shared.

Your Rights

  • Clear all data by uninstalling the extension
  • Export your blocked sites list at any time
  • Disable sync to keep data local only
