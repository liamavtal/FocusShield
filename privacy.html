Overview

FocusGuard is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

Account Data (Optional)

  • Email address - for account creation and login
  • Display name - for personalization
  • Payment information - processed securely by Stripe

Usage Data

  • List of websites you choose to block
  • Focus session statistics (blocks count, focus time)
  • Schedule and settings preferences

Data Storage

  • Local Storage: All blocking data is stored locally on your device by default
  • Cloud Sync (Optional): If you create an account, data can be synced via Supabase
  • Payments: Handled securely by Stripe - we never see your card details

Data We Don't Collect

  • Browsing history
  • Content of websites you visit
  • Personal files or documents
  • Keystrokes or screen content

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: Authentication and database hosting
  • Stripe: Payment processing
  • Google: OAuth sign-in (optional)

Your Rights

  • Export your data at any time from Settings
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Use FocusGuard without creating an account
